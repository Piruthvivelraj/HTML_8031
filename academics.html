SRM Valliammai Engineering College, located in Chennai, offers a diverse range of academic programs, including 11 undergraduate and 9 postgraduate courses in Engineering, Technology, Management, and Computer Applications. The college is known for its robust infrastructure, which supports hands-on learning through workshops and various extracurricular activities, fostering a well-rounded educational experience for students. Accreditation and Recognition The college has been granted Autonomous Institution status by UGC, New Delhi. All eligible courses are Permanently Affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. Several departments, including CSE, ECE, EEE, MECH, and IT, are accredited by NBA, New Delhi. The institution has received a highest “A” Grade accreditation from NAAC. It holds 2(f) & 12(B) Status from UGC, New Delhi. Recognized by DSIR-SIRO, Ministry of Science & Technology, Govt. of India. Accredited by TCS, CSI, and National Cyber Safety & Security Standards. Rated ‘AA’ by IIT Madras for NPTEL Local Chapters across India. The college is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified institution. Vision and Mission Established with the noble vision of “Educate to excel in social Transformation” since its inception on September 9, 1999. Operates under the auspices of Valliammai Educational Society, which has been providing quality education for over three decades. Upcoming Events The 20th Graduation Day of SRM VEC is scheduled for March 16, 2025 (Sunday), highlighting the college's commitment to celebrating student achievements.
